About Me

Hi, I'm Noor Fatihah, also known as Amelia or Amy. I'm a passionate graphic designer, brand identity designer, copywriter, and video editor (for advertisement) with over six years of experience. I specialize in creating unique visual identities, impactful presentations and documents, and engaging content for social media and marketing. My goal is to elevate brands through creative storytelling and innovative design.


I have developed proficiency in graphic design, video editing, and brand identity design, utilizing tools such as Canva, Figma, PowerPoint, and MS Word. My work centres on producing visually captivating designs that effectively convey a clear message. In addition, I possess strong copywriting abilities, enabling me to create engaging content that connects with diverse audiences across various platforms.


During my involvement in the Digital Journalism Club at school, I created award-winning posters, yearbook layouts, and presentations praised for their creativity and clarity. I was honoured with the title of Best Digital Poster at my school. My school is one of the Microsoft Showcase School (Incubator).


Each project I undertake is driven by my passion for visual storytelling. I strive to merge aesthetics with practicality in my designs, aiming to produce impactful work that blends visual appeal with effectiveness.


As I continue to grow in my career, I'm excited to collaborate with brands and individuals who value creativity and innovation. I'm always looking to push boundaries and expand my skill set.


Whether you need a fresh design, engaging content, or just want to discuss ideas, feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out. Let's create something amazing together!
